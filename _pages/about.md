Thank you for visting my site!

I am an Associate Professor in School of Computer and Electronic Information at Nanjing Normal University. Before join NNU, I was an Associate Professor at Jiangnan University and a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at University of Waterloo. I obtained my PhD degree from Queensland University of Technology. 

I investigate topics in the fields of machine learning(deep learning) and signal processing. 

I am particularly interested in speech/audio analysis, time series analysis, and environment/ecology/biology applications.
